Our project

Move towards clean energy

Wind Power

La Estrella Wind Farm project involves the development of NCRE generation plant based on the use of the wind resource in the sixth Region of Chile.

Clean Energy

Consider the construction and subsequent commissioning and operation of up to 20 wind turbines with a total installed capacity of up to 50MW.

Project

La Estrella Wind Farm project is being developed entirely by the company “Eolica La Estrella SpA”, owned by Maxagro.

Developing

The land where the project (1,000Ha) is developed, is owned by Maxagro.
